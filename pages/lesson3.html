Warm-up: Secret Word (This is a h2 tag)

Have students follow the algorithm to discover the secret word.

  1. Look at the first letter of the words. (This is a li tag)
  2. If a letter appears 4 or more times in a row, write down that letter. (This is a li tag)
  3. If the letters are different, ignore the letter. (This is a li tag)
  4. Repeat steps 1-3 for the remaining words. (This is a li tag)
