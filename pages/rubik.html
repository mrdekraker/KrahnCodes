Rubik's Cube Solver

Each move represents 90 degrees clockwise(1 turn)

If there is a ', the direction is counterclockwise. Example: F'

  • U for Up
  • D for Down (bottom)
  • L for Left
  • R for Right
  • F for Front
  • B for Back

Step 1: The Cross

  • The center pieces don't move.
  • The cube is solved layer by layer, not face by face.

Orienting the Cross

R' U' R U R'

Cross center pieces need to be adjusted.
