Teacher Demonstration: HTML Headers

Teacher Demonstration

Show examples of headers using <h1> through <h6> tags. Discuss how different header levels represent the hierarchy of information.

Click Here For Syntax
Activity: Header Practice

Activity

Guide students to create their own headers, using various header tags. Example code:

<h1>Main Heading - Used for Main Title</h1>
<h2>Major Section Heading - Related to Main Title</h2>
<h3>Subsection Heading under Major Section</h3>
<h4>Detailed Subsection under Subsection</h4>
<h5>Specific Detail under Detailed Subsection</h5>
<h6>Lowest Level Detail under Specific Detail</h6>

H1 - Main Heading - Used for Main Title

H2 - Major Section Heading - Related to Main Title

H3 - Subsection Heading under Major Section

H4 - Detailed Subsection under Subsection

H5 - Specific Detail under Detailed Subsection
H6 - Lowest Level Detail under Specific Detail
Activity: CSS Syntax Practice

Activity

Guide students to add their own colors, using the previously created header tags.

<h1 style="color: red">Main Heading - Used for Main Title</h1>
<h2 style="color: blue">Major Section Heading - Related to Main Title</h2>
<h3 style="color: #7FC4D3">Subsection Heading under Major Section</h3>
<h4 style="color: orangered" >Detailed Subsection under Subsection</h4>
<h5 style="color: #ff00ff" >Specific Detail under Detailed Subsection</h5>
<h6 style="color: #1337C0" >Lowest Level Detail under Specific Detail</h6>
